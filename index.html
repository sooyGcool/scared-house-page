scared house

short story

You are a character named Mike. You decide to go camping in the forest at night. It is already late, so you prepare your campsite. It is very late, so you decide to sleep. You wake up to some chilling and terrifying screams. You are very scared, so you decide to grab everything and go to another place. You leave and on the way, you see a mysterious and abandoned house that is a little scary. You decide to explore.
